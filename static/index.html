A tool for crafting and executing conditional promises between two or more parties

This is a guided interface to demonstrate in part the capabilities and tangible ambitions of the underlying immutable logic circuit as encoded in the PromiseMonster smart contract.
Promises are a core primitive in institution building and abstracted coordination. This is an experiment and now one of the ways of using promises to constitute a permissionless promise awarding and executing process.

Some of the Features:


  • Liable entities carry reputation under the form of a address-bounded token
  • Promises are transferable ERC721s and refer to timeframe specific or not future transactions
  • Promises can be made with respect to any asset. Internally an asset is an ERC721 that wraps any other given ERC721 or ERC20 asset.
  • To recap: Promises are NFTs, mostly delegations of future claims to any wrapped arbitrary asset. They are transferable and potentially valuable.
